How to get started

No system before we have a reason to build one.

I do not build a custom system for a coach I have not worked with yet. The onboarding process is designed so both sides know exactly what they are getting into before any long-term commitment is made.

1

Discovery call

We talk through your coaching methodology, your typical client profiles, your brand, and what you need from this partnership. No commitment required.

2

Test project

Two options depending on your preference:

Option A (preferred): I work on your own CV. You go through the full intake process as the client - you see exactly how the research works, how the intake call runs, and what the final documents look like before a single paying client is involved.

Option B: Assign a real client as a test project at a flat $100. You receive the full Package 03 scope - all deliverables, all research, all custom forms and branding materials included. This is not a lite version. It is the complete package at a test rate so you can evaluate the quality firsthand.

3

First 2 to 3 clients

Once the test project is done and we move forward, I start learning your preferences - what resources you use, how you communicate with clients, what your program's standards look like. Each project refines the process.

4

System build - by client 10

By the time we have worked through approximately 10 client profiles together, the full SOPs, templates, and delivery frameworks for your practice are in place. The system is built from how you actually work - not assumptions made before we start.
